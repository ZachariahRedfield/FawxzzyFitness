# Glossary and Standards

## Governance layers
- **Principles:** Immutable philosophy and durable decision heuristics.
- **Patterns:** Reusable implementation approaches.
- **Guardrails:** Enforced or proposed invariants with verification methods.
- **Workflows:** Step-by-step operational execution paths.
- **Reference:** Definitions, standards, and decision records.

## Standards
- Use kebab-case filenames for governance docs.
- Require WHAT + WHY changelog entries for non-trivial changes.
- Track governance semantics through `docs/VERSIONING.md`.
